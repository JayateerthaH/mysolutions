Mapping Business Cases to SDG Targets and Indicators

Air quality monitoring

SDG Target 11.6

It aims to reduce the adverse environmental impact of cities, focusing on air quality and waste management. Air quality monitoring is a key component of achieving this target.

Indicators:

  • Indicator 11.6.2: Annual mean levels of fine particulate matter (e.g., PM2.5 and PM10) in cities (population weighted).

Smart Energy Management

SDG Target 11.6

Smart energy management reduces greenhouse gas emissions by optimizing energy use, promoting renewable energy, and reducing dependency on fossil fuels, which directly contributes to improving urban air quality.

Indicators:

  • not any particular indicator but nearly aligns with Indicator 11.6.2: Annual mean levels of fine particulate matter (PM2.5 and PM10) in cities (population weighted).

Controlled Traffic Systems

SDG Target 11.2

Connected public transport systems—which integrate real-time data, smart ticketing, and communication between transport modes—enhance the efficiency and accessibility of urban transport.

Indicators:

  • Indicator 11.2.1:Proportion of population that has convenient access to public transport, by sex, age, and persons with disabilities.
